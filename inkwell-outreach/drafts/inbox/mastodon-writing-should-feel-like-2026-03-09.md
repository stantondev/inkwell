Writing online should feel like putting something on your own wall in your own room. Not like submitting a manuscript to a machine that decides if anyone gets to see it.

I keep thinking about how personal the internet used to feel. You had a page. You decorated it. People visited because they wanted to.

That's what I'm trying to rebuild. A place where your writing is yours and the only algorithm is "someone chose to follow you."
