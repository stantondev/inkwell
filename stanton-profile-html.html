What I'm building

Inkwell is a social journaling platform. Think LiveJournal meets MySpace, rebuilt for 2026. No algorithms, no ads, no engagement hacks. You own your space and you write what you want.

It runs on ActivityPub, which means your profile is part of the fediverse. People on Mastodon and other platforms can follow you, read your writing, and interact with your posts without needing an Inkwell account.

The code is open source. The product is opinionated. The goal is to build something worth using, not the next social media platform.
